Students Link &
Resources

The internet is often utilized for educational purposes, with various resources being accessed for learning. Online courses and educational materials are frequently explored by students and educators alike. Information is readily available, allowing for diverse learning opportunities to be taken advantage of. The potential for collaboration and knowledge sharing is also embraced within educational communities online.

  • Khan Academy - Free online courses and tutorials.
  • Coursera - Online courses from top universities.
  • edX - Courses from universities and institutions around the world.
  • Quizlet - Study tools and flashcards.
  • Chegg - Textbook rentals, homework help, and tutoring.
  • Wolfram Alpha - Computational knowledge engine for math and science.
  • Studocu - Study materials and notes shared by students.
  • OpenStax - Free, peer-reviewed, openly licensed textbooks.
  • Grammarly - Writing assistant for grammar and style
  • Quillbot - Writing assistant for grammar and style
